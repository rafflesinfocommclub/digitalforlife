Our Vision

Through this website, we aim to educate and spread awareness about the symptoms of stress and how to mitigate it. This is especially due to the increase of Mental Health cases among the current generation. We envision a future where early detection of mental health problems are possible, enabling for quick and effective treatment of the mental illness to mitigate the effects of these problems. We would like to thank Mr Eugene Lim and Mrs Lorraine Neo for guiding us and providing us with the opportunity to make such a website.
