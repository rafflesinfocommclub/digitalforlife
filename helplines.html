Helplines

When experiencing stress for a long period time, it's always best to open up and talk about your problems with a trusted adult or best friend. Additionally, if you don’t feel comfortable talking with parents or friends, there are many trustable and friendly people that have set up helplines just to help people who are stressed out. Here’s some (actually it's quite a lot, there are so many kind hearts out there!):

  • Talk your heart - https://talkyourheartout.com
  • SSI gov - https://iltms.ssi.gov.sg
  • Health hub - https://healthhub.sg
  • National Care Hotline: 1800-202-6868
  • Institute of Mental Health’s (IMH) Mental Health Helpline: 6389-2222
  • Samhealth - www.samhealth.org.sg
  • Samaritans of Singapore: 1800-221-4444
  • TOUCHline (Counselling) – 1800 377 2252
  • Rogerian Psychology - https://rogerianpsychology.com, 86284685
